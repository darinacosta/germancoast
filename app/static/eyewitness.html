Eyewitness Reporting

The Louisiana Bucket Brigade iWitness Pollution Map Project


The Louisiana Bucket Brigade, in collaboration with the Gulf Monitoring Consortium, maintains a database of eyewitness reports and photos of pollution in the Gulf of Mexico region submitted by citizens, government agencies, NGO's, and responsible parties.

The data represented in the current map is a fraction of reports that have been submitted in the parishes of Jefferson, St. Charles, St. John, and St. James. To get a sense of how residents experience heavy industry in the German Coast region, click around on the featured points. This data subset will be updated automatically as new incidents are reported; but for a more complete picture of the Gulf Coast region, please visit the link above, or click here to submit a report.
